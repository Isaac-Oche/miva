About Savage Stitch

Savage Stitch is a contemporary fashion house that dares to challenge conventions. Born from a vision of radical creativity, we blend urban rebellion with high fashion elegance to create statement looks that inspire individuality and boldness.

Our mission is to empower fearless self-expression through edgy, inclusive, and sustainable designs. Whether it’s a streetwear piece or a runway masterpiece, Savage Stitch is fashion that speaks with attitude. We design for those who lead, not follow.

Founded in 2025, Savage Stitch is headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria — a vibrant hub of creativity, diversity, and cultural energy. Our collections are inspired by the raw beauty of African expression and the global pulse of urban fashion trends.

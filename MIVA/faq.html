Frequently Asked Questions

What materials does Savage Stitch use?

We primarily use sustainable and ethically sourced materials, including organic cotton, recycled polyester, and innovative eco-friendly blends. Each product description specifies the exact materials used.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, Savage Stitch ships worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can find more details on our Shipping & Returns page.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn and unwashed items with original tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for detailed instructions on how to process a return or exchange.

How do I find my size?

A detailed size guide is available on each product page to help you choose the perfect fit. We recommend comparing your measurements to our guide for the best accuracy.

Are your products vegan-friendly?

Many of our products are vegan-friendly. We are committed to increasing our range of vegan options. Look for the 'Vegan' tag in product descriptions.
